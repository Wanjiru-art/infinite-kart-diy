About Us

Our story, mission and the people behind Infinite Kart DIY

Our Story

Infinite Kart DIY was founded with a simple belief that everyone deserves to experience the joy of creating art. We started as a small passion project and have grown into a trusted provider of premium paint by numbers kits in Kenya.

Our kits are carefully designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced artists. We believe that painting is not just a hobby — it is a form of self-expression and relaxation.

Our Vision

To be the leading paint by numbers brand in East Africa, making art accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

To provide high quality, affordable paint by numbers kits that inspire creativity and bring joy to our customers.

Our Values

Quality, creativity, affordability and customer satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do.
